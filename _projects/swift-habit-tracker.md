Creates a GUI that runs on iPhone hardware, allows user to create, edit, & delete habits they wish to track their fulfillment of, allows user to mark whether or not they have fulfilled the task within the time frame they set for themselves, tracks the data for the long-term habit fulfillment, displays & visualizes the statistics of a users given habits fulfillment.
